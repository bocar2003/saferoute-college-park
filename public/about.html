About SafeRoute

SafeRoute College Park is a web application made for students near the University of Maryland, College Park. The goal is to make crime and safety information easier to understand through maps, charts, and student-submitted reports.

The app uses the Prince George’s County Open Data crime incidents dataset and connects it to a Node.js Express backend. The project also uses Supabase to store student safety reports.

This project is not meant to replace emergency services. It is only meant to help students be more aware of public safety trends.
